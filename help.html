Help Center

How to Change Your Password

If you need to change your password, follow these simple steps:

  • Go to your profile settings.
  • Click on the "Change Password" button.
  • Enter your old and new passwords.
  • Click "Save" to confirm your new password.
How to Enable Notifications

To enable push notifications, please follow these steps:

  • Go to your settings page.
  • Toggle the "Push Notifications" button to "Activate".
  • Make sure your browser allows notifications.
